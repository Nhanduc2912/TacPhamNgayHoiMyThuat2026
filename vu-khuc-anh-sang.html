Dịch sang tiếng Anh
“Dance of Light” is a visual symphony where color and energy converge in a dynamic rhythm of movement. Streams of light weave through abstract forms, evoking the pulse of life as it continuously evolves and radiates outward. The work celebrates light as a metaphor for hope, emotion, and rebirth, opening a contemplative space where imagination and personal reflection can flourish.
