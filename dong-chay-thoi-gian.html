“Flow of Time” explores the relationship between memory and the present through an abstract composition of interconnected forms and dynamic lines. Each element evokes fragments of experience, layered and transformed by the passage of time. Rather than depicting time as a linear progression, the work reflects its deeper nature as a force that accumulates, reshapes, and preserves the essence of human identity and collective memory.
